PopPUNK: Population Partitioning Using Nucleotide K-mers

PopPUNK is a tool for bacterial genomic epidemiology, capable of rapidly calculating core and accessory distances, clustering genomes, assigning new genomes to cluster schemes, and producing visualisations.

PopPUNK is developed by John Lees and Nicholas Croucher.
